# Holiday Sales Comparison (Year vs Year)

What this project is
I analyzed sales data from one holiday across two different years to compare how product categories performed.

## Goal
To understand how sales changed year over year and which product categories improved or declined.

## Questions I explored
- Which product categories sold the most during this holiday?
- How did sales differ between the two years?
- Which categories increased or decreased in sales?
- Are there patterns in category performance over time?

## What I found
- Some product categories performed better in one year compared to the other
- Certain categories showed growth year over year
- Other categories stayed consistent with little change

## What I used
- Google Sheets
- Sales data
- Basic charts

## Business Impact
This analysis can help a business:
- Understand which products are growing in popularity
- Decide what products to stock more of next year
- Adjust marketing strategies based on past performance
- Identify categories that may need improvement or promotion
